# Pokemon Legends

## Overview

Pokemon Legends is a competitive turn-based puzzle RPG that combines strategic match-3 gameplay with monster collection and tactical battles. Players collect, upgrade, evolve, and battle using powerful Pokemon with unique abilities and elemental strengths.

Each battle features two players, with each player selecting a team of **2 Pokemon**. Every Pokemon belongs to a specific element and role, such as Damage Dealer, Tank, Support, or Healer. The combination of Pokemon chosen before battle greatly influences strategy and win conditions.

The core objective is to defeat the opponent's Pokemon by solving puzzle boards, generating energy, activating abilities, and making strategic decisions during combat.

# Battle System

## Initial Game Player Data

* player have initial 2 pokemon for play game
* player have initial coin for play game

---

## Core Gameplay

1. Select a team of 2 Pokemon.
2. Enter player have 80 HP.
3. Match colored puzzle gems to generate elemental energy.
4. Use energy to activate Pokemon abilities.
5. Evolve Pokemon during battle using Charry energy.
6. Defeat Opponent using our pokemon.
7. Earn rewards, coins, experience, and progression materials.

## Turn Structure

The game uses a turn-based battle system.

### Player Turn

Each player receives:

* 2 moves per turn

A move can be:

* Matching puzzle gems
* Activating a charged ability
* Triggering an evolution
* Using a special battle effect

After both moves are completed, the turn passes to the opponent.

# Energy Generation

Matching gems generates energy for Pokemon.

Pokemon can only attack when enough energy has been collected.

### Correct Matches

If a player matches gems corresponding to their Pokemon's element:

* Energy increases
* Abilities charge faster
* Damage output increases
* Ultimate attacks become available

### Incorrect Matches

If a player matches unrelated gems:

* Minimal energy gain
* Slower ability charging
* Reduced battle effectiveness

Choosing the correct puzzle match is a critical part of strategy.

# Charry Evolution System

Charry is a unique battle resource represented by Orange Gems.

Players collect Charry energy by matching Orange Gems during battle.

## Charry Usage

Charry can be used to:

* Evolve Pokemon
* Unlock stronger abilities
* Increase attack power
* Increase defense
* Gain temporary battle bonuses

## Evolution Benefits

When evolved:

* Higher damage
* Improved stats
* Enhanced abilities
* Increased survival rate

Managing Charry energy correctly can dramatically increase a player's chance of winning.
